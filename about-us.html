About SL Tours

SL Tours is proudly owned by Lisolethu Nkcaza, who is originally from a rural area in the Eastern Cape. He holds a qualification in Tourism and is a professional tourist guide committed to showcasing the beauty and heritage of South Africa.

Our Story and Purpose

Driven by a deep love for South African history, Lisolethu is passionate about sharing it with others and giving every guest an unforgettable experience. Beyond the business, he is also a devoted father and a present husband, and the business name is inspired by both his and his wife’s names.

What Sets Us Apart?

SL Tours stands out for its expert guides who bring South Africa's culture and history to life, tailored travel experiences customized to suit your needs, and a strong commitment to comfort and safety through well-maintained vehicles and exceptional service.

  • Expert Guides: Our guides are not just knowledgeable but passionate storytellers who bring South Africa's history, culture, and natural wonders to life.
  • Tailored Experiences: From half-day city tours to multi-day adventures along the Garden Route, we customize services to meet your travel needs.
  • Comfort and Safety: Travel in our well-maintained vehicles, ensuring your safety and comfort every step of the way.
  • Customer Focused: We go above and beyond to create memorable, hassle-free adventures for every guest.

About Services

SL Tours offers a wide range of travel services across Cape Town and South Africa, from city highlights and cultural experiences to adventure activities, wildlife safaris, and customized private travel packages.

City and Cultural Tours

Guided city tours to major attractions such as Table Mountain, Robben Island, and the V&A Waterfront, including immersive visits to places like Bo-Kaap.

Adventure and Nature

Thrilling experiences such as shark cage diving, hiking, paragliding, scenic Cape Peninsula drives, and wildlife safaris.

Custom Travel Packages

Personalized itineraries designed around your preferences, travel style, and group size for a unique and memorable journey.

Airport Transfers

Reliable and convenient transportation with timely pickups and drop-offs between Cape Town International Airport and your destination, tailored for comfort.
